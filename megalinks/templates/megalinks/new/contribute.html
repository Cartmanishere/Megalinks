• Content

Money is not the only way you can contribute.

This site is useful only because of the content. And that content is dependent on what the members choose to post.

If you are here, you must be aware of the subreddit /r/megalinks where a tonne of great content is uploaded daily.

The goal behind this site has always been to act as a mirror of the content and links that are posted on there.

All this time, I have been the only one backing up that content and publically serving the mirror links. But understandably, many interesting things slip through - that are never backed up. And these great uploads are often lost from the public purview.

The way you can make this site helpful for others is to help post mirror links. Most probably you import the content to your own accounts before downloading. So you can export that from your account and share the new link here. Thus even if original link is taken down, there still remains a chance that the content is not lost.

There is no benefit in posting the original links here.

If you are interested, then Sign Up and you can also post your own mirror links here.
